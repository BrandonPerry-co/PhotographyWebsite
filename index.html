About me

I'm Lando and I'm the most creative Photographer in the southern hemisphere

I specialize in lifestyle family photography. With me being your photographer, I will ask you to get into poses that may seem odd. I LOVE shooting “real” pictures and will ask that you smile, giggle, tickle each other, and show some affection. I love capturing little moments that we sometimes miss and wish they were on film. I started doing this as a hobby while making sure I captured my family's little moments, and I decided to turn this into a career and capture yours as well!
